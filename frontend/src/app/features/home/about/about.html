Our Story

eSHOPPERS was founded in 2020 by two engineers frustrated by overpriced goods and unreliable delivery. With $20,000 in savings and a lot of determination, Sarah and James launched from a Brooklyn apartment with just 200 products.

Within six months they had 1,000 customers — many of whom still shop with us today. We have since grown to 10,000+ products, 500+ brand partners, and a team of 80 people across 4 continents.

We've turned down acquisition offers from larger platforms because staying independent is the only way to stay true to our values. No dark patterns, no hidden fees, no selling your data.

"

We didn't build eSHOPPERS to compete.
We built it to serve.

Every decision is filtered through one question: is this right for our customer?

SM

Sarah Mitchell

CEO & Co-Founder

🌱 Sustainability & Environment

Sustainability isn't a checkbox — it's embedded in how we operate. Since 2022 we've run our data centres on 100% renewable energy and partnered with DHL GoGreen for all long-haul shipments.

Every parcel we dispatch uses recycled or FSC-certified packaging. We banned single-use plastic bubble wrap in January 2023, switching to paper-honeycomb alternatives that are lighter and tougher.

Our Plant-a-Tree programme means every order placed between November and January triggers a tree planted in Kenya or Brazil — 240,000 trees planted so far.
